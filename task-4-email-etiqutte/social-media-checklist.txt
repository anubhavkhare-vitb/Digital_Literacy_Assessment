Social Media Do's and Don'ts (For College Students)

Do's
* Think before you post anything online.
* Maintain a respectful and polite tone in comments and messages.
* Protect your privacy by limiting personal information shared publicly.
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Use social media to build skills, network, and learn new things.

Don'ts
* Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
* Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information.
* Do not engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.
* Avoid oversharing personal details like address, phone number, etc.
* Do not post anything that can harm your academic or professional reputation.
