Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

-->General Safety Tips

* Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
* Do not click on suspicious links received via email, SMS, or social media.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts (banking, email, etc.).
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
* Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details) with anyone online.
* Be cautious of fake job or internship offers asking for money or sensitive details.
* Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering login credentials.
* Limit what you share on social media to protect your privacy and identity.

-->Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
* Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any UPI payment.
* Do not accept unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes.
* Avoid installing unknown apps that ask for access to SMS, contacts, or banking data.

-->Reporting Cybercrime

* Report cyber fraud or suspicious activity immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud reporting.
